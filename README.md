### Sound-Focused Horror Game Prototype

This is a game prototype created as part of my final year dissertation for my undergraduate degree in Games Computing. Achieved a 2\:1 (65%) overall for the module.  

The dissertation focuses on the use of various sound techniques as a primary motivator for gameplay, specifically within the context of horror games. For example:
* Jumpscares - sudden, loud noises usually accompanied by a sudden visual stimuli to provide an immediate burst of fear to the player.
* Sound Effects - any sound that plays as a result of something happening within the game, such as a door opening or a light turning on.
* Ambience - sounds that are non-diegetic in nature; they do not exist due to something within the game causing them, and are only aimed at the player.
* Music - background music (in this context) helps keep the player's tension up (but not peaked) even while nothing intense is happening at that moment within the game.
